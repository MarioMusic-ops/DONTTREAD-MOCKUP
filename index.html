Heritage • Craft • Conviction

Rooted in Freedom. Roasted with Intention.

Don’t Tread Coffee Company was built on principle — a commitment to quality, independence, and the enduring spirit of American craftsmanship. Our beans are carefully sourced from respected growing regions across Africa and Central America, then roasted in small batches to deliver bold, uncompromising flavor in every cup.

We believe in liberty, personal responsibility, and standing firm in what matters. No trends. No noise. Just exceptional coffee with backbone.
